Artist of the Week: Tyler, the Creator


Yes, Tyler is not an unknown artist. But with the recent release of his eigth studio album 'CHROMAKOPIA', his artistic feats obviously deserve the spotlight.

*due to copywrite I couldn't add any songs from the new album, so here's a montage video of Tyler making people uncomfortable*

Tyler Gregory Okonma, known professionally as Tyler, the Creator, once again brings his eccentric and boundary-pushing artistry with his latest album, *CHROMAKOPIA*. Tyler takes listeners on a fresh journey with a more mature sound that marks a sidestep from the experimental chaos of his earlier work. While past albums have showcased Tyler's playful defiance and often abrasive style, *CHROMAKOPIA* feels like the work of an artist who has grown into his own, blending intricate melodies with soulful introspection. This album’s polished production and nuanced lyricism offer a unique depth, revealing Tyler’s evolution as both a musician and a storyteller. Though he retains his signature quirks and offbeat flair, *CHROMAKOPIA* reveals a subtler, more introspective side of the artist — proving that Tyler’s offputting nature is more versatile and complex than ever.
