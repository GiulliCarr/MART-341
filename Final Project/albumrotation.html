Fever: Balthazar

Genre: Alternative Indie
Album Release: 2019

A groove-heavy listen with a prominent bass line, slinky & seductive strings, & an uncanny rhythm that could make your eyes roll back, Belgian rock band Balthazar once again kills it with their fourth studio album.

Glimmer of God: Jean Dawson

Genre: Alternative Indie, Pop/Rock
Album Release: 2024

Released Oct 18th of this year, Jean Dawson makes prominent moves with his third studio album. Dawson's use of ethereal strings, coupled with beats that can only be described as a grimey, addictive 80s funk, creates an experiential album, leaving listeners begging for more.

Division: Jenevieve

Genre: R&B/Soul
Album Release: 2021

A soulful mix of contemporary and modern beats, Jenevieve delivers foot-tapping chords that transport you to a smoky hole-in-the-wall jazz bar. Debut single 'Baby Powder' throws a viciously wicked bass with Jenevieve's sultry wordplay.

Live: Donny Hathaway

Genre: Jazz, Soul
Album Release: 1972

Described as an uncomplicated & energetic set, Live was recorded at two different concerts: side one at The Troubadour in West Hollywood, & side two at The Bitter End in Greenwich Village, Manhattan. Hathaway delivers phenomenal performances, creating an opportunity for listeners to fully immerse themselves into the emotions that come from this smooth, jazzy album.
